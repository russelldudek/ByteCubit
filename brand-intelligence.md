## Company identity
- The official website consistently presents the company name as uppercase `BYTECUBIT`.
- A publicly indexed Bytecubit company profile displays a navy cube mark with a red uppercase wordmark and the tagline `We make IT easy`.
- A crisp, directly retrievable official logo file or official media-kit asset could not be obtained through the available research interfaces. The campaign therefore uses a clearly typeset `BYTECUBIT` identity rather than tracing, redrawing, or approximating the official cube/wordmark.
- Visible identity is paired with `Candidate vision by Russell Dudek` and an independent-candidate statement.

## Color token provenance
- `#111A52` - source-sampled navy from the publicly indexed Bytecubit mark.
- `#C6333D` - source-sampled red from the publicly indexed Bytecubit wordmark.
- `#FFFFFF`, `#101525`, `#EEF2FB`, `#CBD3E7` - neutral/supporting values derived for accessible implementation.
- `#2A91A4` - candidate-original comparison-signal accent used only inside the Signal Twin Workbench.

## Typography evidence and decision
- Public Bytecubit identity uses a bold geometric/technical uppercase wordmark and conventional corporate sans-serif page typography.
- The official typeface and licensing were not publicly identified.
- Implementation uses locally available Roboto for body/UI and Noto Sans Display for headings, preserving a technical, direct, high-legibility character without copying proprietary font files.

## Photography / illustration
- The official site uses broad technology/service imagery and simple service icons. No company photography is necessary to explain this role.
- The campaign uses an original code-native Signal Twin Workbench based on manufacturing application architecture, not stock imagery or a fabricated internal Bytecubit interface.

## Geometry and motion
- Company recognition: white field, navy structure, red emphasis, bold uppercase identity.
- Candidate-original role grammar: paired live/target rails, comparison ring, diagnostic readouts, and one-shot state traces.
- Motion settles after scenario selection and has a complete reduced-motion state.

## Surface plan
- Website: fullest red/navy identity with Signal Twin Workbench.
- Resume and cover letter: restrained red rule, navy section labels, typed Bytecubit identifier.
- Briefs/plans/review: same typographic/rule/table grammar.

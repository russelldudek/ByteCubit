Decision record

Promote

Evidence supports cutover; ownership and rollback are explicit.

Hold

Evidence is incomplete or timing/production conditions are wrong.

Repair

Target behavior, data context, usability, performance, or support needs change.

Retire

The legacy function is not worth reproducing; remove with consumer approval.

Learning return

What component, naming rule, test, diagnostic, runbook, data contract, or governance decision can the next modernization tranche reuse?
